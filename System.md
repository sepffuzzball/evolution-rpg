# The System
*The System* is prevalent throughout the world (though it hasn't always been around, just for a couple thousand years at this point) and is known by all - the System grants special abilities called skills and give numerical statistics in key physical, mental, and social capabilities (colloquially referred to as "stats"), personal status windows, and in some cases personal AIs.

# When do you get the System?
Monstrous races and half-monsters start with the System as soon as they are born and humanoids receive the System at 10 years old (which is also when they can first receive a class and job). Half-monsters at 10 years old must choose whether they want a class or a job, as they can only have one or the other.
