# Tier to Level Ranking
When tiering up, the character's level resets back to 1 (but being such a high tier denotes general power anyhow)

| Tier | Max Level | Title | Details |
|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----|
| 01 | 10 | Starter | The most basic starting tier. Standard humanoids like Elves, Humans, and Dwarves start here. Standard monsters are mundane, such as rabbits, foxes, and wolves.  |
| 02 | 15 | Lesser | The first evolution tier and where some might be granted magic or supernatural abilities. Most humanoids tend to stick with their original race except on rare occasions. Monsters may evolve into something with magic, like a lesser fae or lesser frost wolf. |
| 03 | 20 | Standard | The final tier for the average humanoid, most living in civilization without being in the military will never make it passed here and even some monsters do not have the drive to progress further. |
| 04 | 25 | Greater | More common for those in a combat-based profession or hunter-type monsters, though those that are willing to make it to this tier tend to have enough drive to continue on. Most monster races or humanoid classes that make it to this tier are at least of uncommon variety.|
| 05 | 30 | Superior | This tier represents those that have mastered their craft or there body, further tiers after this become exceedingly rare. At this tier, an individual humanoid or monster are practically untouchable compared to the Standard tier. |
| 06 | 35 | Apex | Tier 6 humanoids and monsters are powerful and start to have their own ecosystem of those at lower tiers due to their capabilities. |
| 07 | 40 | Elite | The Elite or Legendary tier are the one humanoid or monster army, capable of razing entire cities or castles. Those at this tier tend to go down in history books. Rare monstrous races and rare classes are the minimum at this level.|
| 08 | 45 | Ascendant | Those that make it to the Ascendant tier are on their way to Tier 9. Only those with a comical amount of focuse are able to make it to this tier considering how difficult it is to gather experience points at this tier. |
| 09 | 50 | Transcendent | The peak of mortality. Tier 9 is when a character is granted a Domain which is most likened to a subspace bubble that has something to do with their race, class, or job. Epic monster races or humanoid classes are the minimum at this level. |
| 10 | 99 | God | This is the domain of the Gods. Not much is known about this. |
