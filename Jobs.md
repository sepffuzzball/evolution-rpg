# Jobs
Only humanoids and some half-monsters have jobs. These can range from things like "Farmer" or "Cobbler" to "Apothecary", "Alchemist", "Salesman", or "Doctor". All jobs have a rarity associated with them which denotes their general power level.

# Leveling Up
When someone levels up in a job, they receive a bonus to their statistics based on the job's stat-growth ratio, the actual tier record the job is assigned to, and rarity. The tier used here is not the job template's minimum available tier.

Job level-up points: `Tier Job Multiplier * Rarity Multiplier`.

Rarity multiplier:
* Common: 1
* Uncommon: 1.5
* Rare: 2
* Epic: 2.5
* Legendary: 3
* Mythical: 3.5
* Divine: 4
