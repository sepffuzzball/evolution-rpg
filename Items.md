# Items
Items are everywhere and every thing! But not all items are created equally of course.

# Item Rarity
Items of uncommon and above rarity tend to have skills (this could be active or passive). For example, a common shield may just be a defensive item that provides an equipment bonus to Fortitude, but an uncommon shield may also do that as well as provide the ability to set a skill "Bulwark" in this case.

# Item Equipping Stat Limits
A character can have as much equipped as they can physically carry, however they only receive the stat bonuses for an assortment listed below. Of course they should not have 3 pairs of pants that they are using, but the specific locations are kept vague on purpose as different characters will have different amounts of fashion sense...or limbs.
* 3 Armor Pieces (Helmets, Chest Plates, Bracers, Greaves, etc.)
* 3 Accessories (Rings, amulets, etc.)
* 2 Weapons (Swords, bows, gauntlets, claws, holy symbols, etc.)

# Item Skill Setting
Although a character can have as many items equipped as they can in the Limits above, they can only have so many Item Skills "Set" or available for use. The number that they can set is equal to their Tier (so a Tier 06 character can have 6 Item Skills "Set"). This does not preclude them from being able to use the item, they just do not receive the System benefit of the skill.
